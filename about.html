Why am I in EA?

As mentioned above in “Self-introduction”, I decided to join EA mainly because I want to push my limits and go beyond. During my secondary school days, I participated in several S.T.E.M. related competitions and have gained some insights and skills from them. I hope that I can learn new skills as well as more relevant hands-on experience to gain confidence and be better prepared for the working world.

Furthermore, I see the need to apply what I have learnt from not just my diploma course, but from “outside classroom” experience. Therefore, I believe that EA would help to quench my desire to learn new skills, apply what I have learnt, and gain relevant hands-on experience.
